Feedback Intents

What are the Feedback Intents?

  • Advice: Offers guidance or suggestions to aid decision-making.
  • Appreciation: Expresses gratitude for someone's actions or contributions.
  • Coaching: Helps improve skills by providing specific guidance.
  • Criticism: Highlights a perceived shortcoming constructively.
  • Encouragement: Motivates someone and inspires confidence.
  • Evaluation: Assesses quality or value against a standard.
  • Praise: Acknowledges an achievement or quality positively.
  • Psychological Evaluation: Makes judgments about someone's character or emotions.
  • Interpersonal Feedback: Explains how someone's behavior impacts others.
